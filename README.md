# SDP CareerExporter
**SDP CareerExporter** is a Chrome extension designed for Université Laval students to efficiently collect and manage job opportunities from the Service de développement professionnel (SDP) platform.

Once connected to the SDP portal, students can activate the extension to automatically list available job offers and extract key information such as job title, company name, location, and direct links to each posting. With one click, all data can be exported into a clean CSV file, ready to be uploaded into a CRM, ATS, or personal job tracking system.

This tool helps students save time, stay organized, and take a more structured approach to their job search by transforming scattered job postings into actionable data.

**Key features**

* Secure access through the official SDP platform
* Automatic detection and listing of job offers
* Extraction of essential job details
* One-click CSV export
* Compatible with most CRM and job tracking tools

SDP CareerExporter is built for productivity, clarity, and efficiency, giving students full control over their job search data.
